About Sri Maata Degree College

Pioneering excellence in education with a commitment to student success since 2019

Our Story

Sri Maata Degree College was established in 2019 as a pioneer in creating the best academic environment with a proactive attitude and approach to education. Affiliated to VSKU University, we have quickly emerged as a leading educational institution in the Hospet region.

Our college is built on the foundation of providing quality education that empowers students to excel not just academically, but also as responsible citizens. We believe in nurturing talent and building character through rigorous academic programs and holistic development.

With dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and a supportive learning environment, we ensure that every student realizes their full potential and is well-prepared for the challenges of the professional world.
