About Sri Maata Degree College

Sri Maata Degree College is a pioneer in creating the best academic environment with a proactive attitude and approach to education. Established in 2019 and affiliated to VSKU University, we have emerged as a leading educational institution in the region.

Our team of dedicated faculty members, led by an experienced principal, ensures that students not only excel academically but also develop into well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of the modern world.
